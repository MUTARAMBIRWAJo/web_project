COMPANY DESCRIPTION

CROP CARE is a company started in 2022 with the help of Government of Rwanda and it's instution which is in charge of AGRICULTURES in Rwanda also in association with REMA.
We are appreciating the honor of being partner with those instutions and Government of Rwanda. In that case we wish to give rwandan better services in order to make this country to have enough
crop for cash even inside of the country and abroad also home/local consumption crops.
Our Company got the licence of operating in Rwanda after
8 months from the day it is launched with the help of our partner mean On 12/september/2022.

OUR MISSION

As we wish to give much enough to Rwanda our mission is to provide better services to all farmers
who carry out thier farming activities in Rwanda and finding the market of Rwanda in products from farming increas at the rate of 80% in less 5 years by using technology in cultivating,weeding, irrigation and processing
until the time the farmers supply thier products to the market.

OUR VISSION

With the help of our partner we wish to be the best for Rwandan and carry out our activities in all district of Rwanda
that are allocated with agricultural activities as economical activity allocated in these regions and this we wish to achieve it in only 2 years of our working time
means in 2024 every farmer in must know and cooperate with our company.
